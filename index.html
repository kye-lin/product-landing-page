About

Our Mission

Since our founding in 2024, Kobe Mouse has dedicated itself to producing the highest quality cat toys while keeping them affordable for all cat owners across the world. Our mission is to provide all cat owners with great options for cat toys, to keep their furry loved ones happy, healthy and engaged as they grow up.

Play behavior generally emerges in kittens between 2 and 3 weeks of age. - ScienceDirect1

As young kittens grow, they need lots of play time. Even as adult cats, they will benefit from the exercise that comes with pouncing and jumping around with a toy. We have designed our toys to fully engage multiple senses—sight, sound, touch, and smell—to maximize your cat's engagement.
